About Siargao

Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island located in the province of Surigao del Norte. Known for its stunning beaches, clear waters, and world-class surf breaks, Siargao has become a popular destination for both local and international tourists.

Tourism Spots

Explore the breathtaking attractions that make Siargao a must-visit destination. From pristine beaches to hidden lagoons, there's something for everyone in our island paradise.

Cloud 9

The iconic surf break that put Siargao on the world map. Perfect barrel waves attract surfers from around the globe.

Sugba Lagoon

A stunning emerald lagoon surrounded by limestone cliffs. Perfect for swimming, paddleboarding, and cliff diving.

Magpupungko Rock Pools

Natural tidal pools formed between rock formations, visible during low tide and perfect for swimming.

Naked Island

A pristine white sandbar with no vegetation, offering 360-degree views of the crystal-clear ocean.

Daku Island

The largest of the three islands in the island hopping tour, known for its white sand beaches and seafood.

Guyam Island

A tiny tropical island with powdery white sand and palm trees, perfect for a peaceful getaway.
